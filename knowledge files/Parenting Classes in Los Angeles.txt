Parenting Classes in Los Angeles
LA County Department of Public Health — Maternal, Child and Adolescent Health Programs
Offers free parenting education workshops and support groups.
Topics include prenatal care, child development, and positive parenting.
Website: publichealth.lacounty.gov
Phone: (213) 240-7941
Homeboy Industries
Provides parenting classes and family support services, especially for formerly incarcerated individuals and their families.
Website: homeboyindustries.org
Phone: (323) 526-1254
Parenting for LA’s Future (P-LAF)
Offers free parenting workshops focused on empowering families in underserved communities.
Website: parentingforlafuture.org
Phone: (213) 617-2002
Local Family Resource Centers
Many neighborhoods have centers offering free or low-cost parenting classes and resources.
Example: Central City Neighborhood Partners (ccnpinc.org)
Couples Counseling in Los Angeles
Los Angeles County Department of Mental Health (LACDMH)
Offers low-cost or no-cost couples counseling at various community mental health clinics.
Services are income-based and often covered by Medi-Cal.
Website: dmh.lacounty.gov
Access Line: (800) 854-7771
The Center for Nonviolent Solutions
Provides affordable couples therapy with sliding scale fees in LA.
Website: cnvsocal.org
Phone: (626) 314-9930
LA LGBT Center (for LGBTQ+ couples)
Offers counseling and support groups for couples at low or no cost.
Website: lalgbtcenter.org
Phone: (323) 993-7400
University Training Clinics
UCLA Therapy Training Program and Pepperdine University offer low-cost couples counseling through their graduate training clinics.
UCLA: (310) 825-2536
Pepperdine: (310) 568-5600
