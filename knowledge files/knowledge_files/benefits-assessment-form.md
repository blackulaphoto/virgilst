# Social Benefits Programs Eligibility Assessment Form

This comprehensive assessment form is designed to determine eligibility for major social benefits programs based on federal and state requirements. The form covers eight key programs available to California residents.

## Instructions for Use

1. **Complete all relevant sections** - Answer questions for programs you wish to apply for
2. **Provide accurate information** - Eligibility determinations are based on verified information
3. **Gather supporting documents** - Have income statements, identification, and other required documents ready
4. **Consult with case workers** - Final eligibility must be determined by program administrators

## Next Steps

After completing this assessment:

1. **Review your responses** to ensure accuracy
2. **Identify programs** for which you may be eligible
3. **Gather required documentation** including:
   - Photo identification
   - Social Security cards for all household members
   - Proof of income (pay stubs, benefit statements)
   - Proof of residence
   - Medical documentation (if applicable)
   - Immigration documents (if applicable)

4. **Contact program offices** to submit applications
5. **Schedule interviews** if required
6. **Follow up** on application status

## Important Notes

- **Eligibility is not guaranteed** by this assessment - final determination is made by program administrators
- **Income limits and requirements** may change annually
- **Multiple programs** can often be used together
- **Emergency assistance** may be available for urgent situations
- **Appeal rights** exist if denied benefits

## Contact Information

For assistance with applications or questions about specific programs:

- **CalFresh/SNAP**: Contact your county social services office
- **Medi-Cal**: Visit CoveredCA.com or call county offices
- **SSI/SSDI**: Contact Social Security Administration at 1-800-772-1213
- **Housing Vouchers**: Contact local Public Housing Authority
- **TANF**: Contact county social services office
- **WIC**: Find local WIC clinics at myfamily.wic.ca.gov
- **LIHEAP**: Contact local energy assistance providers

This assessment form is based on 2025 eligibility requirements and should be used as a screening tool only. Always verify current requirements with program administrators.
